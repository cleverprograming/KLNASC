Thiru K.L.N.Krishnan - Founder President :

K.L.N. Krishnan born in the year of revolution on 01-01-1918, an Industrialist trained in Japan. He is the founder of Jet Pump Industry, first in India. He runs a number of industries in the field of Pumps, Rubber, Steel, Foundry, etc.,. He is the District Commissioner for Scouts more than 30 Years.Awardee Survaloga Seva ratna awarded by Kanchi Kamakotee Peetam Sankaracharya.He was the Chairman of Apex Association of Indo – Canadian Institutional Co-operation Project sponsored by Government of India in Tamilnadu Polytechnic, Madurai. President of Sourashtra College for 15 Years.Founder President of K.L. Nagaswamy Memorial Sourashtra Polytechnic Council.President of Institute of Socila Science, Madurai.Former Senate Member of Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai.President of Rotary Club of Madurai West and Governor Group Representative.He Continues to serve the society in regard to Education particularly higher Technical Education in the most backward district of Pasumpon Muthuramalingam District in Tamilnadu by bringing a Colleges to serve the society at large.

Dr.K.N.K.Ganesh B.E.,PH.D.(Hons.).,-Secretary & Correspondent

The eldest son of the Founder President is also the President of K.L. Nagaswamy Memorial Polytechnic College. He had the rare privilege of having his Academic and Technical education and training in U.S.A. and in Japan. He is one of the past president of Lions Clubs International. Received the award for "Individual Achievement for Social & Economic Development" at Goa, Organized by Intellectual People & Economic Growth Association, New Delhi on 19-04-2014 He has been conferred with Doctorate Degree by the Universita Popolare di Milano,Italy on 02-02-2014 for his 36 Years of Research in Education and Community Services at Palazo Borghese in the 112th Convocation Ceremony of 113 years renowned University in the world. Received an "Indragandhi Excellence Award" for outstanding achievements in the field of Education from International Business Council, New Delhi on 21-12-2013. Received an "EMINENT ENGINEER AWARD" from Er.R.S.SUNDAR,Site Director & Outstanding Scientist, Kundankulam Nuclear Power Project. Organized by The Institution of Engineers, Madurai Local Centre during 46th Engineers'Day on 15-09-2013 He has been conformed The Honorary degree of Decorate of Letter (D.Lit.,) by the senate of the Univeristy of South America for his dedicated Service and Selfless Contribution to Education.
