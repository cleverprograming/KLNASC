Thiru K.L.N.Krishnan - Founder President :

He runs a number of industries in the field of Pumps, Rubber, Steel, Foundry, etc.,. He is the District Commissioner for Scouts more than 30 Years.Awardee Survaloga Seva ratna awarded by Kanchi Kamakotee Peetam Sankaracharya.He was the Chairman of Apex Association of Indo – Canadian Institutional Co-operation Project sponsored by Government of India in Tamilnadu Polytechnic, Madurai. President of Sourashtra College for 15 Years.Founder President of K.L. Nagaswamy Memorial Sourashtra Polytechnic Council.President of Institute of Socila Science, Madurai.Former Senate Member of Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai.President of Rotary Club of Madurai West and Governor Group Representative.He Continues to serve the society in regard to Education particularly higher Technical Education in the most backward district of Pasumpon Muthuramalingam District in Tamilnadu by bringing a Colleges to serve the society at large.

To Emerge as a Premier Institution in higher education,fostering the development of well rounded individuals equipped with knowledge,skills,innovation,ethics,social responsibility,and a deep sense of patriotism

Mission :

To cultivate an enduring teaching-learning atmosphere focused on quality,while instilling entrepreneurial skills as a guiding principle.   To continuously enhance infrastructure to align with evolving needs and advancements.   To impart essential entrepreneurial skills through the utilization of our own resources and strategic partnership with exemplary organizations.   To foster a culture that upholds the highest values of life, including integerilty,resilience, and ethical conduct.   To actively contribute towards the development of society by nurturing entrepreneurial mindsets and empowering individuals to create positive change.   To instill a strong sense of national patriotism, inspiring students to actively engage in nation-building initiatives and contribute to economic growth and prosperity.

IQAC(The Internal Quality Assurance Cell) :

The main objective of the IQAC is to achieve academic excellence. The IQAC aims at consistent academic accomplishments of the institution. Functions of the IQAC Coordinates the sustenance and enhancement of quality teaching and learning process Works toward the promotion of the holistic development of the students Aims at fostering globally competitive students Conducts programmes for the faculties to facilitate and enrich their knowledge sharing Documents the various programmes to pave the way for quality improvement Ensures the optimum usage of infrastructure and the available resources Disseminates the best practices of quality culture in the Institution Signs MoU with other institutions leading to quality enhancement Collaborates with Alagappa University to conduct various intercollegiate tournaments Hooks up with stakeholders in order to enrich quality initiatives
